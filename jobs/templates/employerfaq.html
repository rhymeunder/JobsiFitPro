How does this all work?

How does this all work?

Qualified job-seekers have uploaded their resumes and/or 30 second video clips

All personal information is meant to be omitted except for their first name.

If you as an employer are interested in interviewing with the candidate,

you can select the employee and JobsiFit will automatically send a message

to the employee with a customized message ID, interview ID, and your contact information.

Basic candidate information will be sent to the HR rep in a email

The interview ID is the employers way of knowing it's the right person.

A invoice will be sent once both parties have verified a successful hire.


What is the difference between "Select employee and send a message" and "Save to favorites"?

Selection vs. Favorites

Selecting "Save to favorites" allows you to save the user for later.

Selecting "Select job-seeker and send message will charge your account"

and allow you to send your automated message with your company details

How much will I be charged?

Selecting "Save to favorites" does not charge your account.

Selecting "Select job-seeker and send message will charge your account"

The following prices are currently being charged

$50 for a successful hire from our website

$25 for every additional hire for that month
