How does this all work?

Employers' Human Resources representitives can login and search for resumes.

All personal information is meant to be omitted except for your first name.

You can see how many companies have looked at your profile from your dashboard

as well as any messages that have been sent to you.

The last resume update and your 30 second video clip (if uploaded) is on your dashboard.

Once you have at least your resume uploaded, you can sit back and relax!

Make sure your notifications have your email and text to get notified when a employer

has sent you a message. The email will contain a message with the company information and

a interview id which you relay to Human Resources when you call. Easy!


What notifications can I receive?

Notifications

Currently we support email and text message alerts when a interview message has been received.

"Notifications" in "Account Settings" can help set it up.
